## How to use Guild 3 Modu Uploader

### How to publish a guild 3 Mod to steam workshop
.\Guild3ModUploader.exe publish --title Silberfurt --description "Silberfurt is a trading and merchant city. Newly founded as part of the Ostsiedlung. The protection of the Teutonic Order and the silver mines make the city flourish into an important trading center." --preview ./content/maps/preview.png --content ./content/

### How to update guild 3 mod on steam workshop

.\Guild3ModUploader.exe update --mod 3232876829 --preview ./content/maps/preview_square.png --changenote "Add better preview image"
